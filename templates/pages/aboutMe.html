ABOUT ME


Welcome! My name is Sinto Ling. I am a data wrangler and analyst. My hobbies include:

  • Softball
  • Badminton
  • Table Tennis
  • Hiking
  • Skiing
  • Camping

I grew up in Ajax, Ontario and attended University of Toronto and Seneca College in Electrical Engineering. Through one of my co-op terms, I realized that I have a much better grasp of working with data and analytics. Thus, upon graduation, I decided to switch to work in the field of data.

Since 2013, for 7 years, I founded, captained and competed in an adult softball team called the Looney Toons. Through this team, I expanded our softball community and all of us became a very close knit group of friends. In 2019, I created a team website and have kept it members-only.

In 2020, I moved to Edmonton for a few reasons. Firstly, I was in search of an enhanced outdoor lifestyle compared to previously - one that involves more adventuring, hiking and skiing in the proximity of the Rocky mountains. Secondly, I moved to Alberta for its lower cost of living. Thirdly, I really liked the small- town feel that this city gives me. Lastly, at the time of writing, I was hoping to land a higher paying job here.
